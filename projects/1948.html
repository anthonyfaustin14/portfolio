1948

Project Description

1948 is a typography poster that celebrates the sans-serif typeface Trade Gothic, showcasing its historical significance and timeless appeal. Created as part of a typography class, the project explores the font's origins, characteristics, and influence while incorporating elements of mid-20th-century American design. By blending historical references, such as Milwaukee transit tickets, with contemporary aesthetics, this poster highlights Trade Gothic's distinctive numerals, strong negative space, and dynamic composition. The project also reflects my personal connection to San Francisco, where the font was originally developed.
