who "understands" any art?

Project Description

who "understands" any art? explores the relationship between text, form, and space through a minimalist 12”x12” format. This project challenged me to create visually compelling designs using only typography, working under strict constraints like a single font size and weight. By iterating on layout, contrast, and texture, I crafted two distinct compositions that showcase the art of typography without relying on imagery.
