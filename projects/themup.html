The Mup

Project Description

The Mup is a Hip-Hop/Rap music blog that highlights three emerging artists from Florida’s music scene. Designed as a digital community space, this blog invites hip-hop enthusiasts to discover new music, explore artist highlights, and browse curated rap-related mood boards. With a focus on South Florida’s unique sound and culture, The Mup immerses users in a digital experience that celebrates the artists contributing to one of the most dominant genres in music.
