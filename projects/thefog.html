The Fog

Project Description

The Fog is a conceptual social media feed inspired by my daily life as a university student in San Francisco. The Fog combines pixel/8 -bit aesthetics with elements of the San Francisco's iconic landmarks, like the Golden Gate Bridge and its famous Karl the fog. This project brings together my personal experiences and my friends, connecting us digitally, regardless of location. By incorporating interactive elements like a "friends navigation tab," I aimed to create a digital space where both friends and strangers can interact with my design.
