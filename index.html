Anthony Faustin

I build identities and web systems with rhythm and restraint. Where clarity, culture, and tactility intersect. Based in Port St. Lucie, FL + open to remote and relocation.

Read full bio

I’m a multidisciplinary designer from Port St. Lucie, Florida, and a graduate of the University of San Francisco. My work merges low-tech graphics, pixel rhythm, and raw texture to create designs that feel tactile, imperfect, and human. Across digital and brand systems, I build experiences that slow the scroll, spark curiosity, and invite exploration. My process draws from music, photography, and nostalgic aesthetics, shaped by the quiet inspiration I find in Florida’s backroads, vintage shops, and late-night games with friends.
